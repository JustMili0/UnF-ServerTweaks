Abilities:
BURNS_IN_DAYLIGHT - Undead behavior + twist: doesn't burn with a helmet on (no damage to the helmet), isn't set on fire when in water but still receives 0.5F damage every 1.2-2s, wearing a helmet negates all burning. Applies only when level.isBrightOutside()
FIRE_IMMUNE - Immune to lava and fire damage, is incompatible with BURNS_IN_DAYLIGHT
FREEZE_IMMUNE - Immune to freezing damage (e.g. powdered snow)
CLIMBS_WALLS - Spider behavior, can climb any wall but has to be at least 0.5 of a block off ground to start climbing
AQUA_GRACE - Additional speed in water
BREATHS_UNDERWATER - Self-explanatory, allows breathing underwater like a fish
CANT_BREATHE_AIR - Starts drowning on land, again, like a fish
CANT_SWIM - Always sinks in water
LIGHT - Permanent hidden Slow Falling 1
SWIFT - Permanent hidden Speed 1
HOPPY - Permanent hidden Jump Boost 1
DWARF - Height always at 0.75 of normal (set to 0.75 if taller than 0.75) or below (can be below if set via custom /scale command), permanent Haste 2 effect
TOUGH - Gives additional knockback resistance, pairs well with STRONG
STRONG - Gives additional 4hp (2 hearts) of attack damage + Scales health with armor (naked = 100hp, full netherite = 40hp)
WEAK_TO_DAMAGE - 1.25x damage multiplier from everything except fall
HUNTED_BY_FOX - Any non-trusting (tamed) foxes will try to attack the player unprovoked within 16 block radius
HUNTED_BY_WOLF - Any untamed wolves will try to attack the player unprovoked within 16 block radius
SCARES_CREEPERS - Cat behavior, scares creepers within a 8 block radius
SCARES_PHANTOMS - Cat behavior, scares phantoms within a 16 block radius
NIGHT_VISION - Can see in the dark, effect only gets applied with 5s duration with everything hidden only when level.isDarkOutside()
HYDROPHOBIC - Takes damage from standing in water, water cauldrons or in rain
FRIENDS_WITH_NATURE - Overworld-native neutral animals (wolves, bees, spiders during day, dolphins, llamas, tarder llamas, polar bears, pandas) will not aggro if hit if hit in any way & has 100% tame chance when taming mobs
IS_MONSTER - Is attacked by iron golems, pillagers don't attack but all other mobs can still aggro and attack
CARNIVORE - Can only eat meat (+honey (bottles) and milk and other misc that aren't plant foods)
VEGETARIAN - Can only eat plant foods (crops, bread, etc) (+honey (bottles) and milk and other misc)
ONLY_EATS_SWEETS - Can only consume foods considered sweet such as sweet berries, glow berries, cookies, cake, pumpkin pie, honey bottles, apples (golden and enchanted too), melon slices
GRASS_EATER - Can consume minecraft:short_grass and minecraft:tall_grass to gain some food points and very little saturation

Ability modifiers:
ADD_GOLD_FOODS_TO_DIET - adds Golden Apples, Enchanted Golden Apples and Golden Carrots to any diet restriction
