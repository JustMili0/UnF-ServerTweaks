BURNS_IN_DAYLIGHT - Undead behavior + twist: doesn't burn with a helmet on (no damage to the helmet),
                    isn't set on fire when in water but still receives 0.5F damage every 1.5s, wearing a helmet negates all burning.
                    Only applies when level.isBrightOutside(), level.canSeeSky() and sky light > 8

FIRE_IMMUNE - Immune to fire, lava, magma block and being on fire damage, incompatible with BURNS_IN_DAYLIGHT

FREEZE_IMMUNE - Immune to freezing damage (e.g. powdered snow)

CLIMBS_WALLS - Spider behavior, can climb any solid wall but only when at least 0.5 of a block off the ground

AQUA_GRACE - Conduit Power effect while in water (BREATHES_UNDERWATER on steroids)

BREATHES_UNDERWATER - Self-explanatory, allows breathing underwater like a fish

CANT_BREATHE_AIR - Starts drowning on land, again, like a fish

CANT_SWIM - Always sinks in water

LIGHT - Slow Falling 1 when falling faster than 0.4 blocks/tick (kicks in before fall damage would occur)

SWIFT - Speed 1 while sprinting

HOPPY - Permanent hidden Jump Boost 1

DWARF - Height capped at 0.75 of normal (players already below 0.75 via /scale are unaffected), permanent hidden Haste 2.
        Note: players with a locked /scale above 0.75 are exempt from the cap

TOUGH - Gives additional knockback resistance, pairs well with STRONG

STRONG - Gives additional 4hp (2 hearts) of attack damage + Scales health with armor (naked = 100hp, full netherite = 40hp)

WEAK_TO_DAMAGE - Takes 1.25x damage from all sources except fall damage

HUNTED_BY_FOX - Any non-trusting (tamed) foxes will try to attack the player unprovoked within 16 block radius

HUNTED_BY_WOLF - Any untamed wolves will try to attack the player unprovoked within 16 block radius

SCARES_CREEPERS - Cat behavior, scares creepers within a 8 block radius

SCARES_PHANTOMS - Cat behavior, scares phantoms within a 16 block radius

NIGHT_VISION - Can see in the dark, effect only gets applied with 5s duration with everything hidden only when level.isDarkOutside()

HYDROPHOBIC - Takes damage from standing in water, water cauldrons or in rain

FRIENDS_WITH_NATURE - Overworld-native neutral animals (wolves, bees, spiders during day, dolphins, llamas, trader llamas, polar bears, pandas)
                      will not aggro if hit in any way & has 100% tame chance when taming mobs

IS_MONSTER - Is attacked by iron golems, feared by villagers, pillagers don't attack but all other mobs can still aggro and attack

CARNIVORE - Can only eat meat (+honey (bottles) and milk and other misc that aren't plant foods)

VEGETARIAN - Can only eat plant foods (crops, bread, etc) (+honey (bottles) and milk and other misc)

ONLY_EATS_SWEETS - Can only consume foods considered sweet such as sweet berries, glow berries, cookies, cake, pumpkin pie, honey bottles,
                    apples (golden and enchanted too), melon slices

GRASS_EATER - Can consume minecraft:short_grass and minecraft:tall_grass to gain some food points and very little saturation

Ability modifiers:
ADD_GOLD_FOODS_TO_DIET - adds Golden Apples, Enchanted Golden Apples and Golden Carrots to any diet restriction
